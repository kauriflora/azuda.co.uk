OO Gauge Track Laying Radius Templates & 67mm Spacers Kit (2nd–5th radius)

Practical track-laying tools for accurate curves and consistent parallel track spacing. This kit is configured to help achieve accurate 67mm spacing on both straights and curves, based on the latest Azuda spacing template design refined during real layout construction.

What’s included

  • 1 × Straight track template (straightener)
  • 1 × 2nd radius curved template – 438mm (17¼")
  • 1 × 3rd radius curved template – 505mm (19⅞")
  • 1 × 4th radius curved template – 571.5mm (22½")
  • 1 × 5th radius curved template – 638mm (25⅛")
  • 67mm double track spacing templates (2⅝") (sold in kit configurations)
Designed for active track laying

These aren’t just static gauges. Integrated handles make them easy to place, slide along rails, reposition, and keep control while forming flexitrack.

Consistent 67mm spacing on curves

Move spacing templates progressively along the track to hold the correct gap through bends, improving clearance and the overall look of parallel runs.

Compatibility

  • Designed for OO / HO gauge (16.5mm) track
  • Ideal for flexitrack and set-track transitions
  • Works with Code 75 and Code 100 rail
  • Likely compatible with Code 83 (not formally tested)
  • Each curved/straight template approx. 200mm length (not radius) for good control

3D printed quality

All items are 3D printed and inspected before dispatch. Minor cosmetic marks or light stringing may occasionally be present and can be removed if required. This does not affect accuracy or performance.
